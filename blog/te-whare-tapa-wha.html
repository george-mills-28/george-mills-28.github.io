Creating a Wellbeing Plan with Te Whare Tapa Whā


Wellbeing Plan Using Tā Mason Durie’s Te Whare Tapa Whā Model

  • Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing): Exercise regularly, eat more vegetables, and stay hydrated.

  • Taha Hinengaro (Mental Wellbeing): Practice daily mindfulness, engage in creative hobbies.

  • Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing): Spend time in nature and connect with a community group.

  • Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing): Strengthen family bonds with regular interactions and support.

  • Whenua (Connection to the Land): Ground yourself by walking barefoot in nature and participating in environmental activities.
