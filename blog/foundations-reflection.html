Reflection on Human Skills Learning


What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the Human Skills learning?

  • I’ve realized how much my ego can sometimes resist asking for help, even when I need it.
  • I’ve learned that being open to feedback improves both my learning process and my coding skills.
  • I discovered that I’m more adaptable than I thought when I let go of my ego and focus on growth.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

Values guide how I approach problems and interact with others. Empathy helps me understand different perspectives, which is crucial for teamwork. Self-awareness allows me to recognize my strengths and areas for improvement, making me a more effective learner and programmer.


What has surprised you the most about the Human Skills learning?

I was surprised by how much these skills influence my ability to learn and work with others. I didn’t expect them to be so closely tied to my success in programming.


What were the most challenging aspects of the Human Skills learning?

The most challenging part was confronting my own biases and ego. It was difficult to stay open-minded and to accept that there’s always room for improvement in how I interact with others.


Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on Human Skills learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

Human Skills are essential for collaboration, problem-solving, and effective communication, which are all key in the tech industry. Understanding these skills helps us become not just good programmers, but great team members.


Does the time you spent studying Human Skills learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead?

No, it wasn’t a waste of time. While technical skills are important, Human Skills are what make the difference in real-world programming environments. They’ve equipped me with the tools to work better with others and to handle challenges more effectively.
