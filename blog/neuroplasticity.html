The Power of Listening and Looping.


Listening and Looping

  • In a world dominated by distractions, the art of truly listening stands out as a rare and transformative skill. Engaging fully with a speaker—without crafting a response—creates a profound connection. The act of repeating back what was said (looping) not only reinforces understanding but also makes the speaker feel valued.

  • Being fully listened to feels empowering and validating, while listening fully brings clarity and deepens empathy. In both roles, the experience fosters genuine human connection, essential for thriving personal and professional relationships.
